The price of our basic package is £25. There is also a refundable £25 security deposit to pay at time of booking to insure us against non-attendance. This deposit is returned to you on the day, upon the conclusion of the photoshoot.

This basic package includes:

A consultation prior to the shoot, to ensure we can deliver the best results to you.

A full photoshoot experience in our custom-designed studio, conducted by a skilled photographer.

The best images from the shoot will be compiled into an album of 50 - 90 shots, so you’ll have plenty to choose from!

Editing of the selected images with state-of-the-art image editing software by an experienced professional, to ensure that your photos meet the highest standards of quality.

A cinematic viewing of your finished album on the same day.

A digital copy of your favorite image on a DVD. This includes the copyright to the image, so you can use it however you see fit. We’re more than happy to provide printing advice on request!
